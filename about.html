About Our Team

Dedicated students of Computer Repair and Maintenance at Centennial College, working together to provide free device repair services.

Our team is composed of passionate students who aim to learn and apply practical skills in computer repair through the ICT Workshop project. We work under the guidance of Professor Will Wong, who provides valuable expertise and mentorship throughout our initiative. This project allows us to gain hands-on experience while helping the community by offering free repair services for computers and devices.

Together, we are committed to growing our knowledge, improving our skills, and supporting those in need through this collaborative effort.
